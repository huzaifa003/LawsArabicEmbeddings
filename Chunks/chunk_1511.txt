Anti-Forgery Law
Chapter One: Definitions and Types of Forgery
Article 1
In this Code, the following terms shall have the meanings assigned thereto, unless the context requires otherwise:
1.Forgery: Any change made deliberately and in bad faith using one of the methods stipulated in this Code to an instrument, seal, mark, or stamp protected by the law, if such change results in physical, moral, or social damage to any natural or corporate person. 2.Seal:  A tool used to stamp documents for authentication or the imprint made thereby.
